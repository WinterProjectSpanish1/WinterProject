    The feast is the most important part of Noche Buena. Some families have adopted an American style meal and serve "Pauo Trufado de Navidad" or Christmas turkey with truffles. Noche Buena feasts vary from country to country, in Mexico, dinner includes homemade tamales, atole, bacalao, etc. Families in the carribean usually eat roast park with rice and beans, pastaues (tomales) and salads. Coquito, coconut flavored alcoholic beverage made with condensed milk, white rum and coconut milk.
