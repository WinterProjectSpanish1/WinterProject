    The ponsettia flower is important to Noche Buena because the shape of the flower is seen as a symbol of the Star of Bethlehem which led the wisemen to Jesus. The poinsettia flower is usually green, but it turns bright red in early winter. The word poinsettia menas the "Christmas Star" or "Christmas Flower"
