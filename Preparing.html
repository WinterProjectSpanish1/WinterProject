    Noche buena is a Spanish celebration that occurs on December 24th also known as Christmas Eve. Latin American cultures and Phillipines celebrate this holiday. For Latin American cultures, this day usually has the biggest feast. Typically, the centerpiece of the meal is roast pork known as lech(special) in Spanish. Alcohol is provided to all members of the feast.
