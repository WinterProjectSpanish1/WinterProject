One common drink made on the night of Nochebuena is tsokolate. This is similar to our popular hot cocoa that is served on christmas day, except this versionwas created in the philippines, so it is also known as filipino hot chocolate.
Another common drink served on nochebuena is a type of fruit punch called Ponche Navideno, which is basically Mexican fruit punch that contains fruits that you might not even be familiar with.
